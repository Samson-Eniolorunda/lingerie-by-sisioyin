Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and more.

Orders

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number. You can also track your order by:

  • Logging into your account dashboard
  • Clicking on the order in your order history
  • Using the tracking link sent to your email

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. After this window, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

If an item becomes unavailable after you place your order, we'll contact you immediately to offer alternatives. You can choose to wait for restock, select a different item, or receive a full refund for that item.

Shipping

We offer the following shipping options within Nigeria:

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days - ₦2,500
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days - ₦5,000
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in Lagos - ₦7,500

Free shipping on orders over ₦50,000!

Currently, we only ship within Nigeria. We're working on expanding our delivery network. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

All orders are carefully packaged in discrete, unmarked boxes. Your items come wrapped in tissue paper and placed in our signature pink dust bags to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unworn items with original tags attached. Due to hygiene reasons, underwear and bodysuits cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

To start a return:

  • Log into your account
  • Go to your order history
  • Select the item(s) you want to return
  • Choose your reason and submit the request

Alternatively, contact our support team for assistance.

Yes! We offer free exchanges for different sizes within 14 days. Simply return your original item and we'll ship the new size at no extra cost. Check our Size Guide to find your perfect fit.

Payment

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Bank Transfer
  • USSD Banking
  • Mobile Money

All payments are securely processed through our payment partner.

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with PCI-compliant payment processors. Your payment information is never stored on our servers. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

You'll be charged immediately when you place your order. If any items are unavailable, we'll process a refund for those items within 3-5 business days.

Sizing

Visit our comprehensive Size Guide for detailed measurement instructions and size charts. You'll find:

  • How to take accurate measurements
  • Size charts for all product categories
  • Fit tips for different body types

If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort. For a more fitted look, size down. Each product page includes specific fit advice. You can also contact us for personalized sizing recommendations.

Our sizing is designed to be true to standard Nigerian/UK sizing. However, different styles may fit differently. Check the product description for any specific fit notes and refer to customer reviews for real-world feedback.

Account

Creating an account is easy! Click the user icon in the header and select "Create Account". You'll need to provide your name, email, and create a password. Benefits include faster checkout, order tracking, and access to exclusive offers.

Click "Sign In" and then "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link expires in 24 hours for security purposes.

Yes! You can checkout as a guest. However, we recommend creating an account to enjoy benefits like order history, saved addresses, wishlist, and exclusive member discounts.
