Size Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive measurement guide and size charts.

How to Measure

For the most accurate fit, take your measurements while wearing only underwear.

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Underbust

Measure directly under your bust, where the band of your bra sits. Keep tape snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your waist, typically about 1 inch above your belly button.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Fit Tips

Between Sizes?

If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort or sizing down for a more fitted look.

Torso Length

For bodysuits, consider your torso length. If you're tall, you may need to size up for length.

Material Matters

Stretchy fabrics like lace and mesh are more forgiving. Cotton items may fit more true to size.

Best Time to Measure

Measure in the evening when your body is at its largest. Avoid measuring right after exercise.
