How to Measure

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

Underbust

For bras, measure directly under your bust where the band sits.

Tip: Measure over your undergarments for the most accurate fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort.
